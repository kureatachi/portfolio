Y-Alert

Y-Alert is a mock-up app designed for international students at Yonsei University, addressing challenges such as language barriers and unfamiliar emergency systems. The app provides accessible, tailored information to help students better prepare for natural disasters.

Bee Yonsei

This project addresses the global decline of honeybees through a community-mapping service for Yonsei University. The platform raises awareness, provides resources and incentives, and fosters collaboration to engage students in honeybee conservation and promote sustainable action.

Origamind

Origami-ND is a mobile application that blends the traditional art of origami with modern journaling, offering a unique platform for emotional expression and self-reflection. By integrating writing and folding, the app transforms personal reflections into visually meaningful creations.

Re-Quest

Re-Quest is a mobile application that gamifies recycling through augmented reality, transforming eco-friendly actions into a fun and rewarding experience. By guiding users to nearby recycling bins with AR animals and providing rewards for participation, the app fosters sustainable habits in an engaging way. Recognized for its innovation, Re-Quest received a Certificate of Commendation from the Institute for Higher Education Innovation.

UX Research Sustainability Social Innovation

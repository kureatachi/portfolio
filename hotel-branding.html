Hotel YUI
Brand Identity  |  Visual Design  |  2024
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Premiere Pro, Figma
Role: Project Leader

Project Overview

This project is a branding concept for a hotel inspired by Osulloc, the South Korean tea brand known for reviving traditional Korean tea culture. The hotel is designed as a space for reconnection, where guests can experience nature, meaningful relationships, and self-reflection, carrying that renewed sense of connection into their daily lives—reflecting Osulloc's emphasis on wellness, harmony, and tradition.

Brand Story

YUI (結) is a hotel that turns every stay into a meaningful connection. In today's fast-paced urban life, genuine connections—with nature, others, and ourselves—are increasingly rare. Inspired by OSULLOC's tea heritage, YUI transforms the hotel experience into a space where these essential bonds can be restored. Every moment at YUI becomes an opportunity to reconnect, leaving guests with a lasting sense of warmth and balance to carry into their daily lives.

Brand Concept and Mission

YUI draws inspiration from the timeless essence of tea culture: the power to bring people together. More than a tea-themed hotel, YUI transforms each stay into meaningful moments of connection, reflection, and warmth that guests carry into their daily lives.

Calendar Design
Application Design  |  Motion Graphic  |  2024
Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Figma
Role: Visual Systems Designer

Project Overview

The calendar highlights plastic pollution, showcasing how pervasive plastic items are in our daily lives. By pairing plastic items with corresponding months, it symbolizes the flow of time and seasons while raising awareness of their impact on our planet's air, water, and soil.

Systematic approach to calendar layout and typography

Design Concept

How might we encourage users to take action against plastic pollution through calendar design, while keeping the experience motivating rather than discouraging?

Solution

Instead of using real images that may feel shocking or upsetting, the design employs positive, motivating, and approachable visuals to raise awareness about one of the world's largest environmental challenges. This approach aims to inspire action without overwhelming or discouraging users.

Motion Graphic

The motion graphic transforms the static print design into a dynamic narrative, using animation and transitions to highlight the presence of plastic in everyday life. While the print design conveys the message subtly, the motion graphic delivers a more urgent and engaging call to action, capturing the audience's attention like an informative and impactful video.

Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the AI-Driven Brand System course, where I explored how AI can be applied in branding. I began with in-depth research on my chosen brand, identified a key challenge, and then proposed a campaign to address it. The final output was a fully AI-generated campaign video, created using various AI tools.

Research & Insights

I began by analyzing Glico's brand positioning, which emphasizes happiness, health, and emotional well-being. Research on the target audience revealed that many young adults in Japan feel a gap between societal expectations and their personal dreams, contributing to declining happiness levels. Given Glico's focus on these values, it was an ideal brand to address this societal challenge through a product campaign, while also tackling the brand's challenge of promoting products beyond their most popular offerings.

Campaign Strategy

The target audience was young adults in Japan who often feel a gap between societal expectations and their personal dreams. The core concept, "Invisible X," encouraged individuals to pursue their personal aspirations, positioning BifiX as a companion on their journey toward happiness and well-being. The campaign was executed through a fully AI-generated video and interactive social media experiences, with AI tools supporting visual creation, storytelling, and audience insight analysis.

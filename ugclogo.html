Club Rebranding Project
Brand Identity | Visual Identity | Logo Design | 2024

Project Overview

Redesigning the logo of Yonsei UIC's biggest international club to better represent its multicultural, inclusive, and globally connected community.

Challenge

The existing logo did not adequately reflect the club's diverse, inclusive nature and global connections. The rebranding needed to capture the essence of a community that brings together students from various cultural backgrounds while maintaining a cohesive visual identity.

Solution

The new logo design emphasizes unity in diversity, incorporating elements that represent global connectivity and cultural inclusivity. The visual identity system creates a strong, memorable brand that resonates with both current members and prospective students.
